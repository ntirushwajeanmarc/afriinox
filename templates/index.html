Background

AFRIINNOX Ltd brings creative and innovative projects to solve the problems that our society, country and the continent are facing. The company’s name “AFRIINNOX” describes what it does, sectors of operation and where its activities are based. In fact “AFRI” signifies that it works from Africa, “INNO” describes it for its Innovative approach while solving societal challenges, and, “X” shows how it does not focus on one field, but rather different fields which need innovative solutions for the communities’ benefit. AFRIINNOX Ltd was registered under reference of the Article 23 of Law N° 007/2021 of 05/02/2021 governing companies, through RDB, this 9th of October, 2023, as a private, domestic company limited by shares.

Company’s Mission and Vision

Mission: “Addressing different societal challenges by bringing new innovative projects across various fields, implementing them into final products to impact the African community”.
Vision: “Being a catalyst in improving the living standards of Africans”.
