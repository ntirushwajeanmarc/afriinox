Welcome to Afriinnox Ltd

At AFRIINNOX Ltd, our main goal is to address societal challenges in different fields, by inventing new tech-innovative solutions and get the final products or services to market for community use. Currently, we work in the most reliant field, Agriculture. We invent new agricultural systems, machines, and equipment that solve agricultural challenges. We also serve as Agri-Data Centre in Rwanda & build agritech capacity through training and consulting private farmers and agriculture cooperatives. Be you a farmer, a potential investor, or a shared mission organization looking forward to working with us, a tech-solutions fanatic, a researcher, a job seeker, or anyone who can eat, we believe that you will find what you are looking forward to getting from here. We do our work maintaining a high level of innovation, collaboration, integrity, and excellence. Please do not hesitate to reach out to our office if you still need further information. Enjoy visiting our website!
