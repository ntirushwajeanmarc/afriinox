Background

AFRIINNOX Ltd brings creative and innovative projects to solve the problems that our society, country, and continent are facing. The company’s name “AFRIINNOX” describes what it does, its sectors of operation, and where its activities are based. In fact “AFRI” signifies that it works from Africa, “INNO” describes it for its Innovative approach while solving societal challenges, and, “X” shows how it does not focus on one field, but rather different fields which need innovative solutions for the communities benefit. Currently, the company operates in the agriculture field, inventing new technological machines, systems, and equipment that solve agriculture challenges. AFRIINNOX also serves as Agri-Data Centre in Rwanda , and builds agritech capacity through training and consulting both private farmers and agriculture cooperatives. AFRIINNOX Ltd was registered under reference to Article 23 of Law N° 007/2021 of 05/02/2021 governing companies of RDB, this 9th of October, 2023, as a private, domestic company limited by shares.
