Afriinnox Agri-data Centre

AFRIINNOX Ltd owns networked agricultural systems, storage systems, and computing infrastructure that are used to collect and analyse amounts of agriculture data in Rwanda. We’re able to assist private farmers, and agriculture cooperatives to understand the environment they’re working in by giving them regional, seasonal, and crop-based data. This guides them to make informed investment decisions based on real data hence increasing high production in the end.

Afriinnox AgritechCapacityBuilding

We, a community of Agriculture Practitioners, Technologists and Farmers, At AFRIINNOX, provide consultancy services about agriculture technologies, and offer training programs to private farmers and agricultural cooperatives about agriculture technology.
